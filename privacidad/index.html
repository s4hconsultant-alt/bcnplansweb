Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

BCN Plans ("we", "our", or "us") operates the @bcn_plans social media accounts on Instagram and TikTok. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our content automation tool.

1. Information We Collect

Our content automation tool is designed exclusively for managing our own @bcn_plans social media accounts. We do not collect, store, or process personal data from third-party users. The tool only accesses our own account credentials and publishing APIs.

2. How We Use Information

The application uses the following data solely for the purpose of publishing content to our own accounts:

  • Our own TikTok creator account credentials (access tokens)
  • Our own Instagram Business account credentials
  • Publicly available news and event information from RSS feeds

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our content pipeline:

  • TikTok API — to publish videos to our own @bcn_plans TikTok account
  • Meta Graph API — to publish posts to our own @bcn_plans Instagram account
  • Anthropic Claude API — to generate captions for our content
  • Unsplash API — to source license-free images
  • Cloudinary — to host images for publishing
  • NewsAPI — to source publicly available news articles

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any personal data with third parties. We do not collect personal data from our social media followers or visitors.

5. Cookies

This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via Instagram DM at @bcn_plans.
